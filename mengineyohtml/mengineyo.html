What MENGINEYO Tracks

Animal Registry

Register every cow, goat, or sheep. Track photos, mother/father lineage, purchase price, breed, and health history.

Daily Milk Records

Record morning and evening milk per cow. Track what went to tea, sold, or wasted. Manager confirms records.

Births & Pregnancy

Record births and newborns automatically become animals. Track pregnant animals and expected delivery dates.

Health & Vet Records

Log vaccinations, deworming, treatments, and sick animals. Get reminders for next vet visits.

Income & Expenses

Track all money in and out. Milk sales, animal sales, salaries, medicine, feed — categorized and reportable.

Reports

Daily milk, monthly summaries, animal stock, finance profit/loss, missing records, and accountability reports.
