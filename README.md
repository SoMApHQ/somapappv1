# SoMAp – Society Management App  

**Educational and Society SuperApp**

**Your all-in-one school management solution.**  
Built to help schools in Tanzania and beyond manage **academics, finance, health, communication, books, and even marketplace features** — all with speed, clarity, and a mission to build society.

---

## 🌍 Vision & Inspiration

SoMAp was born not from imitation, but from a deep need:

- To help schools **operate efficiently**.  
- To support **teachers, students, parents, and administrators** with one connected system.  
- To fight poverty through **quality education and smart technology**.  

We believe technology should serve society — SoMAp is built with that philosophy at its core.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Admissions** – Register and manage student records easily.  
- **Academics** – Record, analyze, and report student results.  
- **Attendance** – Track daily attendance per class.  
- **Finance** – Manage fees, payments, balances, and generate debt/expense reports.  
- **Health** – Keep student health records in one place.  
- **Communication** – Connect teachers, parents, and administration.  
- **Digital Books\*** – Give students and teachers instant access to books, homework, and quizzes.  
- **Marketplace (future)** – Enable schools to connect with suppliers and communities.  

\*Digital Books module is under active development and will expand with SoMAp’s own textbooks and resources.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: HTML5, Vanilla JavaScript  
- **Styling**: [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/) (modern, responsive design)  
- **Database & Storage**: [Firebase Realtime Database](https://firebase.google.com/) + Firebase Storage  
- **Reports & Exports**: jsPDF, AutoTable, SheetJS (XLSX)
